# 🗳️ Executive Disorder

**Executive Disorder** is a satirical political simulation game where absurdity reigns and chaos is law. You play as a wildly exaggerated leader issuing ridiculous executive orders to maintain power, manipulate public sentiment, and survive global meme warfare.

---

## 🎮 Gameplay Overview

Make choices like:
- Ban all birds.
- Declare oxygen a luxury good.
- Replace voting with dance battles.

Each decision affects:
- 🟢 Approval Ratings  
- 🟡 Economic Stability  
- 🔴 Absurdity Meter  
- 🌐 International Reputation  
- ⚠️ Public Panic Index  

Expect memes, mayhem, and meme-generated news chaos, all driven by AI.

---

## 🧠 Powered By AI

- 📰 Dynamic AI-generated news tickers  
- 😂 Auto-generated memes (DALL·E / Stable Diffusion)  
- 🗣️ Satirical press statements (GPT-powered)  
- 🧠 Meme wars driven by AI sentiment systems

---

## 🧩 Features

- 5 playable characters (Corrupt Executive, Meme Politician, Iguana King, etc.)
- Unique strategy paths per character  
- Procedural crisis generation  
- Meme-based diplomacy  
- Multiple absurd endings (AI Coup, Glorious Clownship, etc.)  
- Original art, satirical music, and voiceover comedy
